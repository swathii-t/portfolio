About Me

My coding journey started at the ripe age of 9 years-old, when I stumbled upon the virtual pet website, Neopets. How did it start there? Well, one thing I learned throughout my software engineering journey is that some of the best opportunities come from the most unexpected places. Turns out, there was a whole graphic design community where you can develop websites to host your graphic design business. I was in awe of the artistis creations that people could make, and wanted to have my own little "business" as well. It definitely was a little tough at the beginning, but after doing a little bit of research on HTML, CSS, and GIMP, I had my own graphic design business going on, and that's what started my love of coding and design overall.


After taking a hiatus from coding and trying to figure out some other things I liked, I realized I had a strong liking towards anything that relied on my creativity. I would create music on GarageBand, edit videos of my friends' and my karaoke sessions, and overall just create innovative solutions to problems in my community. This really solidified my decision to be a software engineer to help others all around the world.


I truly believe that the learning journey is a never-ending one. As a current software engineering student at Western University, I've been actively growing through hackathons, clubs, competitions, and professional development opportunities. Besides HTML and CSS, some other languages I have learned along the way are Java, JavaScript, and Python, and I'm looking forward to learning many more technologies and languages throughout my journey.
